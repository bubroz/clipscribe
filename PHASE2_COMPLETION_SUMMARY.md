## Key Decisions Documented

### Business Model (Validated)

**Hybrid Open Source + Paid Service:**
- Free: Self-hosted MIT licensed CLI (unlimited usage)
- Paid: Managed service ($25-500 based on volume)
- Proven model (Supabase $50M ARR, PostHog profitable)

### Pricing Structure (Generous Launch Pricing)

**Tier 0 - Free (Self-Hosted):**
- Open source CLI from GitHub
- MIT license (use commercially)
- Unlimited videos
- Bring your own API keys (~$0.003-0.06/video)
- Community support

**Tier 1 - Single Video ($25-50):**
- 1 video, up to 60min
- All 5 formats (JSON, DOCX, CSV, PPTX, Markdown)
- 24-48hr turnaround
- Email delivery
- No subscription

**Tier 2 - Series ($200-500) - LAUNCH PRICING:**
- **5-20 videos** (VERY GENEROUS vs competitors' 3-5)
- All formats per video + cross-video intelligence
- Series aggregate report
- 48-72hr turnaround
- Priority support

**Tier 3 - Enterprise (Custom, $1000+):**
- 20+ videos or ongoing
- API access
- Custom integrations
- White-glove support
- SLA guarantees

### Positioning Strategy

**Compete Against:**
- Manual analysis (not other software)
- "$25 vs $500-2000 analyst work"
- "10 minutes vs 10 hours manual work"

**Differentiation:**
- Open source (transparency, no lock-in)
- Multi-format exports (unique)
- Series cross-video analysis (unique)
- Honest pricing (show margins)

### Website Strategy

**Pages:** 8 total (5 core + 3 legal)

## Competitive Intelligence Summary

### What We Learned

**Open Source + Paid Works:**
- Supabase: $50M ARR with 90%+ free users
- PostHog: Profitable with 90%+ free users
- Cal.com: Sustainable hybrid model

**Generous Free Tiers Convert:**
- Users trust you more
- Community builds reputation
- 10% converting at high margin = sustainable
- Free users become advocates

**Transparent Pricing Wins:**
- Show exact per-unit costs
- Admit your margins
- No hidden fees
- Builds trust in skeptical market

**Three-Tier is Standard:**
- Free/Starter → Pro/Business → Enterprise
- Clear differentiation
- Easy to understand
- Room for upsell
